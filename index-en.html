Coffee Soundscape

Music is part of the experience: it accompanies coffee, sets the pace of the day, and gives character to the space.

Coffee Soundscape follows the same gesture as our roast: listening, selecting, and caring. Each origin has its own pulse and each cup asks for its atmosphere. That is why we gather playlists rooted in the coffee world and an open selection for those who share it with us.

About Tueste

A lifelong friendship turned into a shared project.

A shared story

Tueste was born from a lifelong friendship and the desire to build a space with a clear identity. Two different paths, one vision: specialty coffee told well, close attention, and a place you remember by how it makes you feel.

Tueste begins

Coffee was a shared passion, always as enthusiasts, and Cordoba became the natural place to return. After years away, they reconnected with the city and fell for it again.

Calle Deanes, in the heart of the Jewish Quarter. A perfect place to start together and bring quality and sustainability to an exceptional setting.

Tueste starts with a clear yet flexible vision. It is early to define exactly how the business will evolve, but Pepe and Ricardo know one thing: they like to surround themselves with good people, with drive, and with the ambition to make Cordoba and Andalusia a mark of quality.

What we want for you

If you are visiting, in the middle of long sightseeing days, we want to give you a few minutes of calm. An oasis where you feel at home.

If you are from Cordoba, we want to open a window to the old town. To invite you to come down and enjoy a space that locals often avoid. If millions visit us, why do we not? We offer you a familiar place to reconnect with your heritage.

For everyone: a place to enjoy quality and sustainability. Where you are welcome and can make this corner your own.

Because in the end, this has always been a shared table. Only now, the chair is for you too.

We will be waiting on Calle Deanes.
